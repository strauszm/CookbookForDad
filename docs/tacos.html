Cauliflower Al Pastor Tacos

This is based on <a href=“https://www.instagram.com/chefrickmartinez/p/C12ItpGLGr4/?img_index=1"Rick Martinez’s great recipe.

Ingredients:
1 package of achiote (annatto) paste
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup orange juice
3 chipotle chiles in adobo, minced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 teaspoon orange zest
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon kosher salt 1 large cauliflower, chopped into 1 inch pieces
1 small can pineapple chunks 1 onion, thinly sliced
1/4 cup lime juice

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 425. 2. Combine all ingredients except cauliflower, onions, and pineapple in a big bowl. Add cauliflower, onions, and pineapple and stir to combine. 3. Roast on two sheet pans pans for 45-55 minutes until the edges are dark and the cauliflower is tender.
